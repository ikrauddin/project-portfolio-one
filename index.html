What is Mindfulness?

Mindfulness is the state of purposefully being in the present moment with full awareness of your thoughts, feelings and surrounding environment.

Practicing mindfulness can help with self awareness, lowering your stress levels, can help you cope with unpleasant thoughts and feelings and most importantly help you be kinder towards yourself.

How to practice mindfulness

The great thing about mindfulness is that it can be done anywhere, at anytime - you can practice mindfulness even whilst doing the dishes!

Below are some tips to help you get started

  • Start with short but regular moments of mindfulness. Incorporate it into your daily tasks such as when you're in the shower or walking the dog. Focus on your thoughts and feelings in that exact moment and your surroundings and senses.
  • Practice in a safe, comfortable environment where you won't find yourself getting easily distracted.
  • Pay close attention to your senses. Really focus on the things you can feel, hear or smell at that moment
  • Take note of where your mind wanders to. Acknowledge the thoughts and feelings that arise and try to accept and observe how you're feeling without judgement.
